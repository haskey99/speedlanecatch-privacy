3. Children's Privacy

Speed Lane Catch is rated for Everyone (PEGI 3). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The game contains no chat or account creation.

All game data (high score, level progress) is stored locally on your device only. Uninstalling deletes it.

5. Your Rights

As we do not process personal data, there is nothing to access or delete. For questions, contact hilyas2799@gmail.com.
